Specifications & quality

What every delivery looks like.

Technical specifications

  • Audio: WAV, 16 kHz (48 kHz studio option), mono, 16-bit PCM
  • Transcripts: verbatim, time-aligned · JSON / TXT / CSV
  • Metadata: speaker age band, gender, region, first language, recording device
  • On request: TTS-grade single-speaker studio corpora

Quality assurance

  • Native-speaker linguists review 100% of transcriptions
  • Inter-annotator agreement ≥ 80%, reported per batch
  • Audio QC: SNR screening, clipping & duplicate detection
  • Free re-delivery on any batch failing agreed QA thresholds

Delivery & SLA

  • Secure S3 bucket or client-specified transfer
  • Catalog licensing: delivery within 5 business days of agreement
  • Bespoke collection: agreed milestone schedule, weekly progress reporting
  • Accuracy commitments defined in each statement of work

Licensing

  • Exclusive or non-exclusive commercial licenses
  • Rights cover commercial use, sublicensing, and AI/ML training
  • Custom terms for enterprise and consortium buyers
  • Full documentation pack accompanies every license
Compliance

Built for your legal team, not just your ML team.

  • Registered with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC)
  • Compliant with the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 and NDPC GAID 2025
  • Written, informed speaker consent covering commercial use, sublicensing, and AI training
  • Full data-provenance and consent audit trail available to client compliance teams
  • Voice recordings handled as biometric data with corresponding safeguards
  • Contractual prohibition on speaker re-identification by buyers
